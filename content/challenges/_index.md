- **[Space Data Centers are Dumb](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-w6G7VEwNq0)**  
  Radiative cooling in vacuum is inefficient, limited to ~100 W/m² due to Stefan-Boltzmann law, compared to kilowatts via convection on Earth. Scaling solar arrays requires prohibitive launch mass and fuel. Cosmic radiation causes frequent single-event effects, corrupting memory and requiring extensive redundancy. Overall, space data centers are impractical for general use compared to advanced terrestrial solutions.
