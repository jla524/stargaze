The exponential growth of Artificial Intelligence has collided with the physical limits of Earth's infrastructure. With power grids maxing out, land disputes rising, and the massive water consumption required for cooling terrestrial data centers, the industry is looking to the ultimate frontier. Space offers infinite, uninterrupted solar power and passive radiative cooling—solving Earth's energy crisis by moving the heaviest compute workloads off-world.
